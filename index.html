Discover the Power of Stories

Presented by Abhirama Sonny, Claire Xiangling Yin, Thalia Hsu, Christian Cruces

Understanding a Single Story

Chimamanda Ngozi Adichie’s TED Talk tells us to not see the world through a single lens. She recounts personal experiences that reveal how narrowly focused stories can form and cement stereotypes.

Appreciating Heritage in Everyday Use

Alice Walker’s short story explores the significance of cultural heritage and its role in family connections. Through the contrasting characters of Dee and Maggie, the story dives into varous themes of identity, heritage, and the bonds that tie us to our past.

Legacies: A Poetic Reflection

Nikki Giovanni’s poem “Legacies” captures the essence of intergenerational relationships and the unspoken bonds that exist within families. It speaks to the importance of preserving memories and the complex emotions that come with growing up and growing old.

Embracing Diverse Narratives

As we explore these narratives, we are reminded of the power and responsibility we hold in telling stories. Whether it's through literature, poetry, or personal accounts, the stories we share shape the way we view the world and each other.
